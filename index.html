About

Sunshine Cafe is a coffee and snack house based in Orlando, FL.

Sunshine Cafe is the perfect place to start your morning. With a warm sunny welcome, Sunshine Cafe offers free wifi and lounge areas for those who want to study or start their online workday with a coffee in hand.

Why Visit Us?

Sunshine Cafe has a unique, team-based approach to our customer service that enables us to offer the best possible quality and experience for our customers.

This quaint, hospitality-driven cafe never fails to wow its patrons with large portions, fresh ingredients, robust flavors, and a little something extra. Sunshine Cafe is an ideal location for a quick business meeting or a quick morning coffee.

Location

Located in Orlando Florida, in a shopping strip, Sunshine Cafe is next to all your essential stores. Stop in for a quick refreshment in between shopping or a speedy morning coffee on your way to work.
